AFE I/O

High speed ADC connection

P1.23	MISO0	SSP0		ADC Data out
P1.20	SCK0	SSP0		ADC Clock in
P1.21	SSEL0	SSP0		ADC CS in

P0.23	GPIO	MUX			Channel selection for ADC
P0.24	GPIO	MUX			2^3 = 8 channels
P0.25	GPIO	MUX			Fast GPIO

Digital pots for attenuation before filtering

P0.1	SCL1	I2C1		IC2 bus for digital pots and accelerometer
P0.0	SCK1	I2C1		IC2 bus

P2.5	PWM1.6	PWM			Speed PWM
P2.4	PWM1.5	PWM			Brake PWM
P2.3	PWM1.4	PWM			Servo PWM

P0.4	GPIO	OTW			Over Temp warning (FET driver)
P0.6	GPIO	SD			Shutdown (FET driver)
P0.2	ADC0.7	I Sense		Current sense (Analog)	

P2.11	EINT1	Speed		Encoder input for speed sensing.
